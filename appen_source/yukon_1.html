Yukon Project | Search Engine Evaluator 

Interested in applying to the Yukon Project? The project is currently looking for Search Engine Evaluators. Evaluators provide feedback and evaluation of various data sets, often by measuring the relevance and usefulness of web pages in correlation to predefined queries, by providing a comparative analysis of sets of results. Your ratings will be used to evaluate search engine quality around the world!  

Responsibilities 

  • Participate in various rating projects based on web search, such as evaluating queries, visiting web pages, and answering questions accordingly
  • Provide ratings representative of people in your country. Familiarity in language and culture in your location is required 
  • Search Engine Evaluators must meet the following minimum weekly production hours requirements:  
    • 10 hours per week non-U.S. Raters  
    • 15 hours per week U.S. based Raters 

Qualifications 

  • Comfortable performing web research 
  • Able to work simultaneously on both a smartphone and computer 
  • Must be eligible to work within the country you reside in (work while traveling outside your country of residence is not allowed at this time) 
  • Strong written and spoken English language communication skills 
  • Ability to work independently with minimal supervision 
  • Detail-oriented 

Other Requirements 

  • You must have a smartphone and Gmail account, and be willing to install an app on your smartphone 
  • Use of an Android phone version 4.1 or higher, Windows phone version 8.1 or higher, or an iPhone version 4s or higher 
  • Possess a high-speed internet connection (Cable Modem, Fiber, DSL, etc.), at least 4-6 Mbps is recommended
  • A clean IP is required with no VPN or proxy connections
  • All candidates are required to take and pass a qualification exam in order to work on the project
  • Study provided guidelines thoroughly  
  • Schedule Exam Start Date 
  • Complete and pass 3-part exam
